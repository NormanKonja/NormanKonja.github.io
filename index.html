About Me

Hi! I'm Norman Konja. I am a computer programmer who specializes in JavaScript, and Python. I've been programming for about 10 years now. I feel like I was chosen to do this for a living simply by the way I was introduced to coding.

The first time I ever laid eyes on computer code I was 14 years old. It all started when I was playing a wildly popular game on xbox live, Call of Duty MW2. I randomly joined a hacked online game that automatically unlocked everything in the game for you upon joining it. I was intrigued by the hacks I was witnessing and wanted to do them myself. So I got to work, absorbing all the information I could find on hacking the game. I eventually found that I could create my own hacks through coding in C++ on a file type called a fast file in an IDE called FFviewer. I taught myself C++ through online documentation and within a few days I had my own custom hacks. One of my custom codes lets you shoot a stealth bomber plane out of your gun, have it do a 360 in midair and explode with special FX. I used my codes to create an income by selling an invite to my games for $10 per person on gaming forum websites. I was 14 years old making $2000 a month selling hacks on a video game. This is what gave me the drive to continue coding for a living, being able to create something out of nothing but a vision.

The reason why I love coding is because I feel like it has a lot of similarities to life in general. You start off with a goal and you make plans to achieve your goal. Along the way to the achievement of your goal you will make mistakes and errors that will need to be taken into account and adjusted to continue further incremental development into the end goal. It takes a strong person to be a computer programmer because it requires constant change and true effort to continue learning and getting better every day. This is the challenging part of coding that I love. There's no better feeling in life or in coding than achieving a goal, espcially when you get to learn from your experiences on the road to achievement!

My interests outside of coding include music, books, cryptocurrency, Jiu Jitsu, investing, learning , and I have an absolute passion for the stock market. I love gaining knowledge and continuously growing in all areas of life. I am a very dedicated person, with a lot of resilience that comes from a will to win and I plan on bringing that same energy with me to whatever organization I end up with!
